Who we are

Ro de Rivero

An interior designer, with studies completed at Toulouse Lautrec in the year 2000, and further studies at FIT in New York. His time in various cities and work for the architecture firm Farewell defined the style that imbues all the projects in which he participates.

In 2012, he returned to Lima, participated in his first edition of Casacor Peru, and a year later founded Studio Ro de Rivero Interiorismo and Monocromo. This project condenses all the expertise acquired during his professional career.

A lover of nature and simple lines, he seeks to incorporate natural elements such as wood, iron, and natural stones in a balanced manner in every project. For Ro, perfection lies in creating timeless environments that transmit sensations.
